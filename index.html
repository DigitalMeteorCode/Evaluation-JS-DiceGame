Roll the dice.
The result will be keep in your current score.
You can roll the dice many times as you want, but play smart :
If the dice hits 1, your current score would be reset to 0 and your turn would end.
Hold your current score to save it in your global score and end your turn.
First player to reach 100 in his global score wins !
