Roll the dice.
The result will be kept in your current score.
You can roll the dice as many times as you want, but play smart:
if the dice hits 1, your current score will reset to 0 and your turn will end.
Hold your current score to save it in your global score and to end your turn.
First player to reach 100 in his global score wins!
